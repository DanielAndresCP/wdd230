Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Chillán Chamber of Commerce, Industries, Tourism, and Services.: This name represents a city in which the chamber operates ("Chillán") and the business types it includes.

Site Purpose

Chillán Chamber of Commerce brings together companies and interacts with trade groups from the Commerce, Industries, Tourism, and Services sectors in the Ñuble region. We empower businesses with talks, seminars, and workshops, and represent them before regional and national authorities.

Scenarios

  • What benefits does my bussiness receive by becoming a member?
  • What businesses are members of this chamber?
  • What are the latest actions the chamber has taken to promote or help businesses on the region?
