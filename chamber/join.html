Membership Benefits and Prices

Non-Profit Membership

Price: Free

Benefits:

  • Electronic Signature for Businesses: Access to both simple and advanced electronic signatures.
  • Barcodes: The chamber is the representative of GS1, the entity responsible for assigning barcode numbers at both national and international levels.
  • Monthly Commercial Bulletin: Clarifications or reports on individuals and companies.

Bronze Membership

Price: $8.000 CLP

Benefits:

  • Everything on the Non-Profit Level
  • Donatello Pizzeria: 10% discount on pizzas at the restaurant, 5% discount on other meals served at the restaurant.
  • Multiservis Ltda.: 8% discount on equipment and machinery rentals.

Silver Membership

Price: $20.000 CLP

Benefits:

  • Everything on the Bronze Level
  • Quezada Siles Optics: 15% discount on list prices.
  • Cerda and Associates: 20% discount on professional service fees, first consultation free of charge.

Gold Membership

Price: $30.000 CLP

Benefits:

  • Everything on the Silver Level
  • Certificate of Origin: We provide guidance and initiate the certification, pre-certification, and verification of the origin of potentially exportable products.
  • Virginio Gómez Professional Institute: 50% discount on enrollment and 20% on the annual fee for members, their spouses, and children, as well as for employees, for all regularly offered courses.
  • Río Viejo Funeral Home: 20% discount for members and their families.
